About Me

My journey in technology started in August 2019, when I’ve graduated with bachelor’s degree and passed interview for an engineering role in Verizon. I was still a student trying to remotely finishing master’s degree, but I was also fully employed at Verizon. It wasn’t an easy task to find balance between university and work, yet I was able to pass CCNA the same year.

After 4 months in my position I’ve actively started learning python to automate my daily tasks to be more efficient with my time. Eventually I’ve created python scripts for a whole team to improve our processes. In 2021 I was able to transition into cybersecurity as well as successfully finish my master's degree.

Leading me to present, where I no longer have to split my focus, time and energy between University studies and work. Now I can fully dedicate my life towards my passion and grow like never before.
